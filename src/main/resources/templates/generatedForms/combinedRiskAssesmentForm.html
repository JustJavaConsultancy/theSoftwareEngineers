Financial Risk

Assessment of financial stability and projections

Medium

Findings: Revenue growth has slowed in the last two quarters. Customer concentration risk with 35% of revenue from 3 clients.

Recommendation: Negotiate price adjustment based on recent performance. Implement customer diversification strategy post-acquisition.

Legal & Compliance Risk

Pending litigation and regulatory compliance

High

Findings: Ongoing patent litigation could impact 15% of revenue if lost. GDPR compliance needs improvement in European operations.

Recommendation: Structure deal with escrow for potential litigation costs. Budget for compliance upgrades in first year.

Operational Risk

Integration challenges and operational continuity

Medium

Findings: Different tech stacks may complicate integration. Key personnel retention is a concern.

Recommendation: Develop detailed integration plan with milestones. Implement retention bonuses for critical staff.

Reputational Risk

Low

Findings: Strong brand alignment with complementary customer bases. No significant PR issues in company history.

Recommendation: Develop joint marketing campaign to announce acquisition to customers.

This acquisition carries moderate risk primarily due to legal and financial concerns. The upside potential is significant but requires careful mitigation planning in these areas.

Recommended Mitigation Plan

  1. Price adjustment: Negotiate 10-15% reduction based on recent financial performance and litigation risk
  2. Escrow account: Hold back 20% of purchase price for 18 months to cover potential litigation costs
  3. Integration team: Assemble cross-functional team 90 days before close to plan operational integration
  4. Retention bonuses: Implement 2-year retention packages for key engineering and sales personnel
  5. Compliance upgrade: Budget $2M for GDPR and security compliance improvements in first year
